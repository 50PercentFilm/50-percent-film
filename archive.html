Curated Selection — Free to Watch

The Archive.

A hand-picked collection of some of the best short films ever made — all publicly available to watch right here, for free. The Archive sits alongside our own catalogue as a celebration of the form: Academy Award winners, festival favourites, and the viral shorts that quietly reshaped the internet. This isn't our work — it's a starting point. If you've never properly sat down with short film before, this is where to begin.

All films remain the property of their original creators and are embedded directly from YouTube. Use the filters below to browse by genre.
