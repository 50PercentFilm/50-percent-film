Synopsis

A washed-up detective chases a homeless outlaw to restore his glory.

Jesse van Moof, a washed-up and broke detective, becomes obsessed with a newspaper story about the "Crazy Cowboy," a homeless outlaw who ambushes couriers and steals their food. Convinced this bizarre criminal will restore his glory, Jesse builds a case and prepares a trap. When his plan fails, he stumbles upon the Cowboy by chance and chases him to his hideout, only to discover a laughing, wordless drifter who cannot even explain himself.

Director

Emre Karabacak is a Turkish artist based in Amsterdam, working across photography, video, sculpture and design. He treats the visual language of his work as seriously as words, making art to express rather than impress, pieces meant to linger and quietly stir. Toy-Gun carries that instinct into moving image.
