Our Belief

Half the
time.
All the
feeling.

Short films are the most honest form of cinema. No padding, no filler — every frame earns its place. They are where directors take their greatest risks, where new voices speak loudest, and where the medium remembers what it's actually for.

50% Film exists to close the gap between exceptional short filmmaking and the audience it deserves. We curate with intention, platform with care, and believe that a 14-minute film can leave you just as changed as a two-hour one.

Every film we accept is listed on Letterboxd, published with full directorial credit, and treated as the serious work of art it is. We take submissions from anywhere, pay attention to everyone, and answer to nothing but quality.

What we look for
  • Any genre. Any subject. We care about quality of vision, not category.
  • Minimum 1080p resolution with clean audio. Technical craft matters.
  • Any runtime. From a few seconds to 40 minutes — length is no barrier.
  • Rights must be held by the director. Music cleared or original.
  • You keep 100% ownership. We take a non-exclusive licence to host and promote.
  • Accepted films are listed on Letterboxd and promoted across our channels.
