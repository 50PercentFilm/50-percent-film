Synopsis

"Still here."

In a city that never stops moving, one man tries to keep up with everything. Set against the backdrop of relentless noise and invisible pressure, Death by a Thousand Cuts is a quiet reckoning with the weight we carry, the masks we wear, and the cracks that begin to show when no one's looking. A visceral exploration of burnout, silence, and survival — where nothing explodes, but everything hurts.

Director

Riley Bowen

Writer / Director

Riley Bowen is a UK-based filmmaker and co-founder of 50% Film. Death by a Thousand Cuts is their latest short, a quietly devastating portrait of modern burnout and the invisible toll of keeping pace with a city that never slows down.
