Questions

Before you send.

Does it cost anything to submit?

No, and it never will. No entry fees, no tiers, no paid “priority” review. Submitting is free for everyone.

Do I keep the rights to my film?

Yes, completely. We only ask for a non-exclusive licence to host and promote it. You keep full ownership and can license it anywhere else, anytime.

Can I submit a film premiering at a festival?

Yes. We won't do anything that compromises a festival premiere. If your film needs to stay offline for a window, tell us and we'll work around it.

What genres and lengths do you take?

Any genre, any runtime, any country. We care about the strength of the vision, not the category it fits into.

How long until I hear back?

Within 14 days. We read every submission personally, and if it's a no, we'll tell you why.

Can I take my film down later?

Yes. Email us and we'll remove it within 14 days, no questions asked.

What do I get if you accept it?

A dedicated page on the site, a Letterboxd listing, full credits, and a place in the catalogue. You're also in the running for the Vol.01 anthology.
